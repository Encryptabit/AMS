<context>
The major work this session was fixing the performance issue where /proof/overview took ~1 minute to load. Root causes:
1. ChapterManager.CreateContext was deserializing book-index.json on EVERY call, even though it was already cached
2. BlazorWorkspace.SelectChapter was disposing chapter handles on switch, forcing re-creation

Fixes applied:
1. ChapterManager now checks `_bookContext.Documents.BookIndex` before deserializing
2. BlazorWorkspace now maintains `_chapterHandles` dictionary, returns cached handles
3. Overview page caches computed metrics in `CachedBookOverview`

Result: 1.2s load time (acceptable for 20+ chapters with lazy hydrate loading)
</context>
